The following types and sizes of discounts are provided to students on a paid basis

Students of the 1st year from the 2nd trimester and 2-3 courses who have successfully passed the examination session (with grades A, A-, B+, B, B-) can qualify for a discount of 30% for the next trimester.

Students enrolled in the 1st year

The term of study at AITU is 3 years. Our students graduate from the university 1 year earlier, due to the intensity of their studies. AITU students study until July, thereby closing more loans.

Undergraduates

Undergraduates enrolled in the 1st course on a paid basis are provided with the following types of discounts:
  • Holders of a diploma with honors of the previous level of education - 30%
  • Holders of a diploma of the previous level of education with a GPA of 3.6 and above -20%
  • Graduates of the University who entered the Master's program - 30%

How to enroll in Astana IT University?

1 Take the AITU Excellence Test (AET) entrance exam
2 Score at least 70 points for the UNT (at least 5 points for each subject)
3 Register in the ABITU admission system

Documents required for admission

  1. Certificate or diploma (original)
  2. Appendix to the certificate or diploma (original)
  3. UNT Certificate
  4. Identity card (copy)
  5. Photo 3x4 (6 pcs.)
  6. Reference form 075 (original) + fluorography (images)
  7. 063 certificate - copy (vaccination card)
  8. The identity card of one of the parents when submitting documents for a child under the age of 18.
  9. A copy of the child's birth certificate when signing an agreement on the provision of educational services by the parents of a child under the age of 18.
  10. If available, IELTS/TOEFL IBT/TOEFL ITP/APTIS/Interpress certificate (Almaty, Nursultan and Karaganda) (copy)
  11. Certificate of attribution (for young men)
  12. Application (*You need to print out and fill out in advance before applying to the university*) for applicants to bachelor's degree programs [Download]
  13. Contract in 2 copies (It is necessary only after the actual receipt of the grant or the actual enrollment at the university on a paid basis)

The duration of study at AITU?

The term of study at AITU is 3 years. Our students graduate from the university 1 year earlier, due to the intensity of their studies. AITU students study until July, thereby closing more loans.

What is the cost of studying at university?

The cost of studying at our university is 2 million tenge per year, excluding discounts.

Do you have a hostel?

Yes. In 15 minutes from the university there are three student houses for boys and for girls, in the EXPO Residence residential complex.

In what language is the training conducted?

Education at Astana IT University is conducted entirely in English.

Is it possible to enter a university with MESC scores?

Astana IT University has a military department. Our students can get the rank of reserve officer in 5 specializations: Cybersecurity, geoinformation systems, the use of topogeodetic units and units, psychologists, automated command and control systems.
They can also get a reserve sergeant, in 3 specializations: Topogeodesia, staff, electronic computers.
You can enroll in the military department, both on a grant and on a paid department.

Is it possible to enter a university with MESC scores?

You can enter the university with MESC scores, provided they are converted into UNT scores. You can carry out this procedure on the basis of the National Testing Center.

Is it possible to enter a university with SAT scores?

You can enter the university with SAT scores, provided they are converted into UNT scores. You can carry out this procedure on the basis of the National Testing Center.

What is the threshold score for admission to the grant?

There is no threshold score for admission to the grant. To apply for a grant, you need to score at least 70 points, as well as at least 5 points in each subject for the UNT and pass the AET entrance exams. Further grants will be distributed by points. First of all, the applicants with the highest results receive grants.
